# KD CM Notes: Instruction Guide

**Welcome to KD CM Notes.** This tool is specifically designed to streamline the workflow for Case Managers at KD. While Salesforce (SF) is a powerful platform, its expansive UI can sometimes lead to repetitive, mundane tasks that slow you down. **KD CM Notes bridges this gap**, helping you bypass temporary UI challenges and get up to speed instantly so you can focus on what matters most: your clients.

---

## 🏛️ Core Functionality
The five functional pillars that power your daily workflow.

1.  **Client Notes:** The central hub for tracking your claim’s journey. Grasp every situation at a glance and know exactly what is pending or required next.
2.  **Fax Forms:** Generate professional, pre-filled PDF forms (Letter 25, Status to FO/DDS) in seconds, ensuring accuracy across all submissions.
3.  **Data Parsing:** Eliminate manual entry and copy-pasting by automatically extracting medical provider details and IR report data into structured tables.
4.  **Workflow Automation:** Speed up routine outreach and task management using personalized templates for NC Letters, Emails, and SMS messages.
5.  **Scheduler:** Manage your timeline with an integrated calendar that tracks holidays, client revisits, and custom reminders.

---

## 📝 Client Note Workspace (`Alt + 1`)
- **Information Panel (`Alt + Q`):** View essential demographics like SSN, DOB, and contact info. Click **Fetch** (`Alt + E` / `Alt + F`) to sync data directly from SSD Apps.
- **SSA Contact Panel (`Alt + W`):** Find Field Offices (FO) and DDS branches. Use the **Map View** (📍) to identify offices with shorter wait times.
- **Matter Panel:** A read-only overview of filing dates, claim statuses (Initial/Recon), and "Prior To Request" (PTR) alerts.
- **Dynamic Formatting:** Highlight text to access the formatting toolbar. Start a line with `>` for quick to-do lists.
- **Follow-ups (📅):** Set follow-up dates via the **Revisit** button; you will be reminded automatically on that date.

---

## 🏥 Medical & Medication Panels
### Medical Provider (`Alt + 2`)
- **Auto-Parsing:** Data from SSD Apps is automatically parsed into the table. Click **Raw** to toggle the source view or edit the table directly after parsing to ensure accuracy for future faxing.
- **Expansion:** Press `Enter` on the last row to add new provider entries.

### Medication Manager (`Alt + 3`)
- **Predictive Search:** Find drug names instantly by typing just the first few characters—no more asking clients to spell every character.
- **Organization:** Categorize entries by prescription, condition, or supplements. Drag-and-drop to reorder.

---

## 🦾 Specialized Automations
- **Mail Resolver (`Alt + A`):** Speed up record-keeping—open a mail log that needs resolving and press `Alt + A` (or click the resolution button) to automate the process instantly.
- **Fax Forms (`Alt + 4`):** Preparation area for outgoing PDF documents. Review and edit fields before exporting.
- **IR Tool (`Alt + 5`):** Specialized report parser that extracts facility names and addresses directly from the page.
- **Automation Tool (🤖):** Floating panel for triggering **NC Letters (NCL)** and applying communication templates.

---

## 🌐 Workspace Features
- **Interactive Taskbar:** Sticky bar at the bottom showing active panels and daily "Matters touched" counters.
- **Dynamic Theming:** Note colors shift automatically based on the client's local timezone.
- **The Dashboard (`Alt + T` / 📝):** Search through all saved notes, configure settings (⚙️), and manage your data backups.
- **Global Note (`Alt + ~`):** Multi-tabbed sidebar for persistent data like Attorney IDs or EINs.
- **Instructions (`Alt + H`):** Access this guide quickly within the Global Notes sidebar.
